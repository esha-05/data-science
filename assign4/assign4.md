# Statistical Analysis Report

##  Objective
To determine whether study time significantly affects exam scores.

## Findings

### 1. Correlation
There is a positive correlation between study hours and exam scores, indicating that students who study more tend to score higher.

### 2. Visualization Insights
- The scatter plot shows an upward trend, confirming a positive relationship.
- The histogram indicates how scores are distributed among students.

### 3. Hypothesis Testing
- The p-value is less than 0.05, which means the result is statistically significant.
- We reject the null hypothesis.

## Conclusion
Study time has a significant positive impact on exam performance. Increasing study hours is associated with higher exam scores.
